Professional Experience

Google

June 2023 - September 2023
Software Engineer Intern, Kirkland, WA

During my tenure as a Software Engineering intern at Google, I had the privilege to contribute to the enhancement of Google Cloud Platform's Meet application. My primary focus was the implementation and rigorous testing of back-end functionalities, where I spearheaded the development of a Timer service using Remote Procedure Calls (RPC). This involved enabling users to seamlessly create, set, initiate, halt, reset, delete, and fine-tune multiple timers within meetings, all while ensuring efficient communication between the client and server. To ensure optimal performance and reliability, I architected the system to store timer data and metadata utilizing Google's Spanner database service. My role demanded adept utilization of internal Java async libraries and injection frameworks to impeccably manage RPC request operations, exemplifying my commitment to delivering innovative solutions within a complex and dynamic environment.
